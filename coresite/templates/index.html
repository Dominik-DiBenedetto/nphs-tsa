About Us

If you are looking for a fun and engaging way to spend your after-school hours, North Port High School TSA is the club for you! Our club provides a welcoming atmosphere that is perfect for anyone with a passion for engineering, problem solving, and creative thinking. Here, you can find like-minded people who share your interests while having fun and learning something new every day. Our team of dedicated advisors are always on hand to mentor, teach, and support students, ensuring they achieve their full potential.

Our chapter is dedicated to achieving the best results, providing a supportive environment for all members, and striving for excellence in all aspects. With a focus on innovation and creativity, we aim to make a positive impact in our community and beyond. Watch us in our pursuit of excellence and become a part of our outstanding team.

North Port High School TSA is an excellent chapter that combines hard work and fun. Our members are dedicated to the TSA motto which is "Learning to lead in a technical world". TSA helps our members learn not only the fields of science, technology, and mathematics but how to lead the next generations into the future. We strive to create a supportive and inclusive community where everyone can thrive.

When the middle school students of Heron Creek and Woodland complete their middle school TSA journey, most of them will be able to attend North Port High School TSA. We are excited to welcome them to our school and look forward to helping them continue their TSA journey.
